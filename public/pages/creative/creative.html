My Creative Projects

I love being a designer, but I also love expressing my creativity in different ways. Here are a few different projects I've worked on recently. Some are video editing, some are music, but they were all a lot of fun to create!

Last Concept Video: HCI for Startups

Concept Designer, Actor, Video Editor

This was a fun concept video I made with a partner for an HCI class; the prompt was to create a concept video for a new email client. Our product summarized emails to make it easier for busy people to read through them and give more time to themselves.

Last Acapella Performance: Treblemakers

Beatboxer, Video Editor

Over the pandemic, I edited the music videos for my acapella group. The last two videos were "Dancing With the Devil" by Demi Lovato and "Side" by Pentatonix. This year, our group won 2nd place in ICCA Quarterfinals and I won outstanding vocal percussion!

Last Document Design: PODS Case Competition Document

Document Designer

For a case competition hosted by the American Marketing Association, I designed the case document we submitted to be adjudicated. The document was made with Adobe InDesign and the cover page was designed in Adobe Illustrator. I made a few different possible cover designs and then designed the case document based on the cover. While it's not perfect, it was a great learning experience for both cover and document design!

Last Percussion Performance: Flying Colors

Percussionist (Vibraphone)

Over the pandemic, I was able to learn a mallet percussion duet with another CMU student. We played "Flying Colors" by Adam Tan. It was a lot of fun to get back into in-person music!

About

My name is Stephen Tao and I'm a sophomore at Carnegie Mellon University majoring in business administration with an additional major in interdisciplinary human-computer interaction. Currently, I'm looking to dive into a UX design role.

I discovered my passion for UX design from my business endeavors. I was really interested in marketing; more particularly, the aspects of consumer behavior. This was how I transitioned into one of my first projects: Meranti, a marketing management platform for makers. As I discovered the creative applications of my theatrical and musical passions, I knew that UX work would be the perfect opportunity for me to explore, iterate on, and solve problems through design. I quickly became enchanted by HCI and now appreciate the importance of functional, aesthetic, seamless design.

I still, however, continue pursue my creative interests on the side. I'm a comedic improviser, a beatboxer, theatrical performer, musician, and more. Check out some of my clubs/interests below to learn more about me!
